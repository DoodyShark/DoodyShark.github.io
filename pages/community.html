Featured in NYUAD's Undergraduate Research Report for Summer 2021-2022

Summer 2022

My research project from Summer 2022, KATIB, was conducted in the Applied Interactive Multimedia (AIM) Lab with Professor Eid's guidance and gained recognition in the NYUAD Undergraduate Research Report. We created various serious games designed to enhance different skills and offered various levels of challenge. This initiative was intended for use in educational and healthcare settings, allowing schools and hospitals to utilize it for student learning and rehabilitation of individuals recovering from strokes.
